Family Party

Just like Christmas Eve in the west, family in China get together to enjoy an annual dinner on the New Year Eve. And in the following days, especially the first day of the new year, all the family members sit down enjoying the valuable leisure moment at home. The elderly might prefer drinking tea and chatting on the family or social issues, while the middle-aged might set up a gambling by playing poker or mahjong.
